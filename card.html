2bopp tape

Double-sided tape with polypropylene carrier. Use for mounting and repairing works of all types. The tape has good adhesion and initial tack to wide variety of surfaces including polar and non-polar materials, except plasticized PVC.

  • Good resistance to UV radiation.
  • High heat resistance.
  • High resistance to gasoline, kerosene, alcohols, solvents.

Properties

  • Carrier: BOPP Film
  • Type of adhesive: Synthetic rubber
  • Release-liner: paper
  • Color: silver

Optional

  • Length: 25/ 50/ 100 m
  • Width: 60 – 1050 mm

Technical data

Наименование
Peel adhesion Afera 5001A 10 N/cm
Loop tack PSTC-16 10 N/cm
Tensile strength Afera 5004 35 N/cm
Alongation at break Afera 5004 60 %
Application temperature +5…+75 (±5)°С
Working temperature -30…+95 (±5)°С

Application

  • Printing
  • Production of household appliances

  • Installation and construction work
